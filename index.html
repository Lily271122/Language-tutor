What do I teach?

I teach Russian and my classes are for

  • adults of all levels from complete beginners to advanced learners (levels A1 to C2)
  • bilingual children whose parents want them to learn to read and write in Russian
  • native English children not speaking Russian at all
  • teenagers preparing for their Russian GCSE exams
  • those advanced learners who wish to take their TRKI exams
  • actors interested in the Russian accent and/or Russian phonetics

I teach English and my classes are for

  • adults from complete beginners to advanced learners (levels A1 to C2)
  • those preparing for their IELTS or CPE exams
  • those wishing to improve their pronunciation (I specialise in Phonetics!)
  • those wishing to take their Life in the UK exam
  • Russian-speaking kids who need help with their English at school
